Introduction to Internet Of Things

The Internet of Things (IoT) refers to a network of interconnected devices , objects , and systems that collect , share , and exachange data through the internet. These "things" are embedded with sensors, software ,and other technologies, enabling them to communicate and interact with each other and with humans, often in real time.

Applications of IoT
